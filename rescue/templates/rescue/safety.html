Summary of my learnings in managing covid. See what you can apply. Though everything I say is based on my reading of scientific literature and results of Artificial Intelligence predictive models I am not a doctor so please listen to your doctor if he contradicts anything I say.

The virus is airborne and whatever precautions you take you still run a risk of contracting the disease. So do all you can to minimise probability by wearing a mask and washing your hands before touching your face. If you must interact with others try to do so outdoors. If indoors keep air purifiers on because they circulate air through hepa filters. Also keep exhaust fans on wherever available.

If you have to take a flight the most dangerous place is the airport and in the aircraft before take off and after landing. During the flight air is circulated through hepa filters and a mix from outside air so you are relatively safe.
