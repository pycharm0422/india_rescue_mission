Give Oxygen and Save Covid Afflicted India

India is facing an unprecedented humanitarian crisis in the form of covid calamity. The healthcare system is teetering on the verge of a total collapse. Responsible citizens are taking up the challenge of saving the covid stricken, as the hospitals and healthcare infrastructure is extraordinarily burdened. Deaths and mourning have become a daily reality. The crisis is unparalleled because the world's second largest population, developing country infrastructure and scarcity of resources like hospital beds, oxygen manufacturing facilities and unpreparedness on the part of government have left large swathes of people to fend for themselves, or at the mercy of compassionate citizen volunteers. We gave risen to the call of duty to protect our fellow citizens. We are organising a drive to procure supplies of oxygen, oxygen concentrators, medical supplies, and grassroots reach to the nooks and corners of India. We are a network of doctors, Wharton and IIT alumni, coming together to rescue India from this devastation to whatever extent we best can. We solicit your support in donations. We will account for every donation, every rupee spent, and record the recipient details. Together in India Covid Rescue Mission, we will attempt the impossible. Let us save lives. TIME IS OF THE ESSENCE. SO forgive our sloppiness.
